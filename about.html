About

Welcome to Adopt a Fan! This website was created to make sure visitors and sports fans can fully enjoy their experience when traveling to see sports events.

Use this platform to invite new friends and other Adopt a Fan users to your local bar or regular restaurant, inform others about where you are supporting your country or even where you are hanging out. On the next pages, you’ll able to create your invitation so that you can “adopt” fans and exchange experiences.

Stay tuned - very soon you’ll be able to ask a local questions in real time about the city you are in. And in a few weeks, our app will be ready and it will be even easier to make new friends!



Welcome to Adopt a Fan! We hope you enjoy your experience!
